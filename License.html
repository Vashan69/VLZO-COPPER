THE OPPORTUNITY

Bangladesh imports over $800M in copper annually. No entity currently aggregates copper at scale domestically. Demand is growing at 12-15% per year. VLZO Engineering has the land (27+ acres), the technology (VLZO Coin blockchain), and the network (64 districts) to become Bangladesh's definitive copper supply infrastructure.

🎯
The Problem

Bangladesh's copper supply chain is fragmented, import-dependent, and inefficient. Industrial buyers pay premium prices for inconsistent quality. $800M leaves the country annually. No domestic aggregator exists at scale.

⚙️
The Solution

VLZO aggregates copper from all 64 districts, stores and grades it on 27+ owned acres, processes it to industrial specification, and delivers reliably to 1,000+ B2B buyers — at lower cost than imports.

The Returns

28% IRR · 3.2× return multiple · Break-even Year 4 · $560M projected Year 5 revenue · 6% equity or 4% structured loan available · Backed by 27 acres of land.
